Key Regulations:

  • IMO (International Maritime Organization): Global maritime safety and security standards
  • SOLAS (Safety of Life at Sea): Minimum safety standards for ships
  • MARPOL (Marine Pollution): Prevention of pollution from ships
  • MLC (Maritime Labour Convention): Crew welfare and working conditions
  • ISPS (Ship and Port Facility Security): Security measures
